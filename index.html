Marketing Proposal

Core BEE Advisory (Pty) Ltd
A structured 3-month content plan designed to build authority, increase engagement, and generate B-BBEE advisory enquiries through LinkedIn and Facebook.

Proposal Overview

This proposal outlines a 3-month social media strategy for Core BEE Advisory, with 2 posts per week across LinkedIn and Facebook. The objective is to position the business as a strategic B-BBEE advisory partner, educate the market, increase audience engagement, and support lead generation through professional, informative, and brand-aligned content.

24 Posts Total 2 Posts Per Week LinkedIn + Facebook Educational + Strategic
